Another Character: +$10 for chibi. +$20 for illustrations, portraits, and comics.
Another character for comics does not count how many times they will be drawn. it will only be how many characters show up in the entire comic. ex. there are two characters but they show up in every panel. it would only be +20 for that second character.

All further contact will then be through Artistree DMs. (updates, WIPs, sketch approval, etc.)
